# Project 1 — StraightTalk Wireless BA Case Study

## Overview
End-to-end Business Analysis case study for 
StraightTalk Wireless — a prepaid telecom platform.

## Business Problem
Manual service management causing high call center 
volume and customer frustration across 5 key 
service flows.

## What I Did
- Stakeholder analysis — 8 groups
- Documented 5 service flows end to end
- 40 functional and non-functional requirements
- 12 user stories with Given/When/Then criteria
- Complete RTM — SIT, UAT, Production tracking

- RTM documentation best practices
